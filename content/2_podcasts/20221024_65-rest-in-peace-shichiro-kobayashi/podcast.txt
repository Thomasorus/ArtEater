Title: 65: Rest in Peace, Shichiro Kobayashi

----

Text:

A chill episode after our couple months hiatus. We use this time to celebrate and discuss the life and work of Shichiro Kobayashi, the massively influential background artist and art director of a mind-blowingly long list of anime masterpieces and classics such as Cyborg 009, Sally the Witch, The Gutsy Frog, Samurai Giants, Aim for the Ace, Lupin III: The Castle of Cagliostro, Berserk, Revolutionary Girl Utena, Space Adventure Cobra, Urusei Yatsura 2: Beautiful Dreamer, Venus Wars and tons more.
