**SPOILERS - The Boy and The Heron will be discussed at length and spoiled. Go watch it first! (or not)...**
Richmond, James and Mike Judge(@goro_toro) have an impromptu podcast immediately after coming out of the latest Hayao Miyazaki film, The Boy and The Heron. The crew explores the film's artistry, structure and Miyazaki's influences and development as a director coming into possibly his actual last film.
