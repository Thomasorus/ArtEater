Title: 40: A Dreamcast Discussion with Jörg Tittel & Professor Andy

----

Text:

Richmond and Professor Andy are joined for an intimate discussion about The Sega Dreamcast with Jörg Tittel - Director, Writer, Producer of Oiffy and now making VR game, The Last Worker and cinematic event, A Winters Journey. Learn about the last console that Sega made, its early history, the unique launch titles, its role Japan's internet infrastructure and its influences on the Indie video game scene.
