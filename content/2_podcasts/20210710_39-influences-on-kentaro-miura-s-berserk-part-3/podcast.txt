Title: 39: Influences on Kentaro Miura's Berserk (Part 3)

----

Text:

We continue discussing Kentaro Miura, the creator of Berserk who recently passed away. Rutger Hauer movies, Miura's personal stories, ideals and caricatures, Sam Raimi fandom plus real historial coincidences, Persian symbols, Guin Saga and more.

We discuss the various influences on Miura as he developed Berserk over the years.

This will be Part 3 of our Series of episodes on Berserk.
