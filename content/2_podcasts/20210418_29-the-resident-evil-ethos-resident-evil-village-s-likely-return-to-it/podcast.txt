Title: 29: The Resident Evil Ethos & Resident Evil Village's Likely Return to It

----

Text:

Recurring guest, James Stanley (@Beefy_Kunoichi) returns with Richmond and Shawn to discuss the various teasers for Resident Evil Village and discuss the influences and ethos of the Resident Evil Series as its evolved over time. Crazy mansions, Shinji Mikami's influences, the impact of Noboru Sugimura's Tokusatsu writing, Chris Redfield's character and buffness plus speculations on RE Village.
