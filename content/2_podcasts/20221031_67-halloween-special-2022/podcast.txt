Title: 67: Halloween Special 2022 ft. Gianni Matragrano

----

Text:

The Art Eater crew find themselves walking in a howling storm through a dark wood when they come upon a totally not haunted mansion. Taking shelter inside, the group looks for a way out of the mansion and they are joined by special guest, Gianni Matragrano, a voice actor and all around spooky dude to talk about their scariest horror moves, comics, books, media and games as they pass the time searching.
