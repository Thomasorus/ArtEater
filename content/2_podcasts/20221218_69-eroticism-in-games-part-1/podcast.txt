Title: 69: Eroticism in Games (Part 1)

----

Text:

The Art Eater crew maturely discussions eroticism in games for our 69th Episode. We discuss early games with sexual depiction in them as well as how censorship of sexual content has changed in the industry plus discussion of the range of approaches in games such as King of Fighters, Baulder's Gate, Mortal Kombat and Hades.
