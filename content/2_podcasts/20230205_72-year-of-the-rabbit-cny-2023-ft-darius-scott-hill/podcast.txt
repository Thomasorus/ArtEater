Title: 72: Year of the Rabbit - CNY 2023 ft. Darius Scott Hill

Text:

We have a full house today with the Art Eater Crew and are joined by special guest, illustrator and animator, Darius Scott Hill (@burntmoth19) to celebrate Lunar New Year 2023, The Year of the Rabbit...by discussing Rabbits in games and media. We discuss the fighting game, Rabbit, Brutal: Paws of Fury, Peter Rabbit, Armello, Overgrown, Super Turbo Atomic Ninja Rabbit, Waku Waku 7, Sonic, Odin Sphere and more. SO MANY Rabbits. Celebrate with us.
