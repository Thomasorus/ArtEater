Title: 73: Dead Space Remake & Lovecraftian Horror (Part 1) ft. Derek Swinhart

----

Text:

We are joined today by game journalist and writer, Derek Swinhart to talk about the Dead Space Remake and as well as modern survival horror. We get into allegorical themes, Lovecraftian influences, Callisto Protocol parallels, the important changes in the remake and the unique dismemberment mechanics in Dead Space.
