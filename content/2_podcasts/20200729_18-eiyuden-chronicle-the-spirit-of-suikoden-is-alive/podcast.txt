Title: 18: Eiyuden Chronicle, The Spirit of Suikoden is Alive!

----

Text:

Shawn gushes about the DNA of Suikoden I and II games while Richmond along with recurring guest - Part Time Shuffle creator, James Stanley (@Beefy_Kunoichi) dive deep into trailer and screens from the newly announced Eiyuden Chronicle by Rabbit & Bear Studios, a team of original Suikoden designers and other JRPG creators. We discuss the excitement behind who this team is and how cool it is that they are bringing an epic and fantastic classic JRPG built with modern design sense to reality.
